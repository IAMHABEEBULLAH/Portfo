Key Principles of Gymnastic Ethics

Respect

Promoting mutual respect among athletes, coaches, and officials to create a positive environment.

Fairness

Ensuring fair play and equal opportunities for all participants regardless of background or ability.

Integrity

Encouraging honesty and ethical behavior in all aspects of gymnastics.
